What Is Sustainability

Sustainability is the ability to sustain or continue a process over a long amount of time. Sustainability in both political and business contexts aims to keep natural or physical resources from being destroyed so they can be used for a long time to come.

How can we make our environment cleaner

What we can do to make our environment cleaner is to reduce the usage of our electrical appliances, drive your car less, recycle the waste products, reduce carbon footprints, grow your food locally, save energy, plant more trees, and use more public transportation.
